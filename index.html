Welcome to Allfire Design

Allfire Design is an independently owned wet fire design company, based in Brisbane, and servicing the whole of Queensland.

Our ethos is simple. We design Deemed to Satisfy fire sprinkler and deluge systems to code requirements and have been doing so since 1982.

We have grown from a single operator to a design office utilising approximately 10 personnel comprising of both permanent staff and a small number of sub-contractors. All permanent staff have been trained in-house and attend external courses to keep skills and knowledge fresh. Our business has grown gradually and steadily over the years simply through word of mouth, and we are very proud of this fact.

We are happy to assess and quote for any client requirements, and our services include:

  • Design layouts
  • Tender drawings and specifications
  • Hydraulic calculations
  • Block plans
  • Evacuation plans
  • Pipe work fabrication sheets
  • As Installed drawings
